Job Descriptions

Business Associate

Description

Business Associates are responsible for providing traditional consulting services to Optimir's clients.They use their business knowledge and skills to help clients solve a wide range of problems. When staffed on projects, Business Associates can expect to populate models, analyze data collected from primary and secondary research, and provide insight into business decisions for clients.

Qualifications

- 3.5 GPA or higher strongly preferred
- Critical thinking and analytical abilities
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills




Technology Associate

Description

Technology Associates combine business strategy with technical expertise to provide software advisory services to our tech clients. In short, they are hybrids of software engineers and business consultants. Technology associates can expect to assist tech clients by working on back front end code for their products and services, or designing UI material for websites. They can also expect to provide advisory knowledge to Optimir Business Associates and Executive team members.

Qualifications

- Knowledge of a programming language such as Python, Ruby/Rails, Java, etc.
- Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript preferred
- 3.0 GPA or higher strongly preferred Critical thinking and analytical abilities
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills
