Optimir Consulting was founded in 2011 by three UC Berkeley students passionate about business strategy and growth. Optimir is a non-profit consulting group that provides innovative, sustainable, and substantial improvements for various industry clients in the start-up world. Our consultants come from a variety of academic disciplines and experiences and are dedicated to providing results-driven solutions. As a student start up, we are committed to developing and expanding our consultants' skills. We offer fast-paced and unique training for our consultants in order to optimize our clients' operations through implementation. Compensation for our services may be claimed as charitable and tax-deductible under the Internal Revenue Service, Code 501(c) 3.
