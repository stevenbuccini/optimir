About Optimir

Optimir Consulting is a student-run consulting group and registered nonprofit that provides innovative, sustainable, and substantial improvements for various industry start-ups. Optimir Consulting was founded in 2011 by three UC Berkeley students passionate about business strategy and growth. Our consultants come from a variety of academic disciplines and experiences and are dedicated to providing results-driven solutions. As a student start-up, we are committed to developing and expanding our consultants' skills. We offer fast-paced and unique training for our consultants in order to optimize our clients' operations through implementation.
